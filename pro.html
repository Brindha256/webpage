DOG BREEDS

Golden Retriever

Golden Retrievers are excellent pets due to their affectionate and obedient nature. The Golden Retriever is a breed of retriever dog that originated in Scotland.

  1. Group: Sporting Group Height: 20-24 inches Weight: 55-75 pounds

Rottweiler

The Rottweiler breed is renowned for its great strength. Rottweilers are extremely loyal and dedicated to their owners. Rotties is a common nickname for the Rottweilers breed.

  1. Group: Utility Group Height: 24-26 inches Weight: 90 - 110 pounds

Great Dane

Great Dane is frequently known as the "Apollo of Dogs." It is a large-sized dog breed that originated in Germany.

  1. Group: Working group Height: 30 - 32 inches (male), 28 - 30 inches (female) Weight :110 - 175 pounds

German Shepherd

The German Shepherd is a breed that almost everyone knows. It is a bold dog breed that is loyal.

  1. Group: Working group Height: 24 - 26 inches (male), and curious. Weight: 55 - 75 pounds

Beagles

Beagles are very friendly and smart dogs. It is the world's most adorable DOG.It is a small with a sensitive nose that makes it excellent at detecting scents.

  1. Group: Hound group Height: 13 - 15 inches Weight: 20 - 25 kg

Pug

Pug is one of the world's oldest dog breeds. Pug dogs are among the friendliest dog in India and are renowned for their unusual physical characteristics.

  1. Group: Toy Group Height: 11-13 inches Weight: 15-18 pounds
